Educational Technology & E-Commerce (E-TeC)

A Resilient Digital Learning Platform Empowering Teachers and Transforming Classroom

The Problem

Across many schools, especially in low-resource and emerging economies, teachers struggle with low and irregular salaries and limited income alternatives, inadequate access to affordable and user friendly digital platforms aligned with classroom practice, scarcity of quality interactive learning materials, lack of simple tools for learners assement and progress tracking, limited oportunities for professional development and digital upskilling.

The Solution

E-TeC is a resilient and user-friendly digital learning platform that supports teachers and enhances learning outcomes by offering curriculum-aligned interactive content, assessment and progress-tracking dashboards, simple class management tools, professional development and digital skills training, and new income opportunities for teachers through tutoring and content creation

How It Works

1. Create Profiles

Students and teachers access secure academic profiles.

2. Learn & Assess

Stakeholders manage learning activities and assessments digitally.

3. Track Progress

Performance is monitored in real time.

4. Improve Outcomes

Data-driven insights improve teaching and learning.

Key Features

  • Student/Teacher academic dashboards
  • Teacher assessment and progress tracking
  • Curriculum-aligned resources
  • Secure user profiles
  • Interactive tools for learners

MVPprototype completed– platform deployment pending funding.

Early Validation

The prototype has received positive feedback from educators and learners. Several Stakeholders have expressed interest in pilot implementation once the platform is deployed.
